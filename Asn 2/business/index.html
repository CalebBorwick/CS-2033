About Us

The office is a paper sales company featured in Scranton, Pennsylvania. With branches in Albany, Utica, Scranton, Akron, Nashau, Buffalo, Alert and so much more we provide office supplies with exceptional service. We pride ourselves in helping the working world work better with solutions that deliver industry-leading products. Like our wise CEO once said, “Limitless Paper in a Paperless World”. We sell everything and anything you will need to run a successful, well-organize and respected office space.

Team

John Jameson

CEO

John Jameson is an American Business executive, an industrial engineer, a philanthropist and a father of 4. Jameson is the highest-ranking executive in The Office, whose primary responsibilities include making major corporate decisions, managing the overall operations and resources of a company, acting as the main point of communication between the board of directors and corporate operations and being the public face of the company.

Michelle McAdams

COO

Michelle McAdams is a Canadian tech entrepreneur, philanthropist, and a mother of 8. Graduated from Western University with her Ph. D. in Biological Chemistry. Most widely known for her clean and sustainable energy program while working with the Canadian government. McAdams oversees the company’s business operations and reports to the CEO. McAdams ensures the company has effective operational and financial procedures in place.

Lilly Bribe

CDO

Lilly Bribe is a Jamaican born and raised entrepreneur, businesswoman, entrepreneur, investor, and mother of 4. Graduated from Harvard University with a Masters in corporate law. Bribe is known for her work with the endangered animals of South America. As the CDO Bribe is a senior executive who bears responsibility for the firm's enterprise wide data and information strategy, governance, control, policy development, and effective exploitation.
